Loring greenway

Loring greenwayis an urban walkway that connects Nicollet Mall with Loring Park, the city park that gives its name to the Minneapolis neighborhood ranked 2nd most livable by Twin Cities METRO magazine. If you aren’t familiar with it you might not even realize it’s there… but this urban walkway is a hidden treasure that gets heavy use by thousands of people every day.
